452.215-73 Post Award Conference.

As prescribed in 415.570, insert a clause substantially as follows:

Post Award Conference (NOV 1996)

A post award conference with the successful offeror is required. It will be scheduled within ______* days after the date of contract award. The conference will be held at: ______*.

(End of clause)

* Contracting officer to insert number of days and location.
